OUR SERVICES

The Dental Clinic believes in Conservative treatment as opposed to radical treatment.
Each patient will be seen, examined with care and treatment options will be given with all pros and cons of each treatment.
We listen to our patients. Patients can decide which treatment option would be best suited.

Restorative Dentistry

Add tooth bonding or chairside dental veneers

Oral surgery

Removal of teeth, surgical removal of impacted teeth.

Implantology

placement of implants. Straumann, Nobel and Dentium, immediate implant placement treatment completed within one week.

Endodontics

Root Canal treatment for teeth which cannot be saved other than for extractio

Pediatric dentistry

Care for toddlers and children and adolescent teeth.

General dental parctice

check up and clean up.

Prosthodontics

Porcelain/Metal crowns, acrylic and metal Dentures, porcelain/ceramic veneer placement to correct cosmetics or misaligned, protruded teeth.

Orthodontics

Correction of crooked or/and protruded teeth by use of fixed appliances- metal / ceramic / clear braces or removable appliances and retainer.

Geriatric Dentistry

Care for over 70s dental needs.
